Who We Are ?

Accelor Microsystems Environment Test Laboratory (AMETL) is a specialized division of Accelor Microsystems Private Limited, located in Mohali, near Chandigarh. In June 2025, AMETL began offering its services to external clients, with IS/ISO/IEC 17025:2017 (NABL) accreditation currently in progress.

Backed by a skilled team, AMETL tests electronic products to national and international standards including IS, IEC, JSS, and MIL,ensuring accurate, reliable, and compliant results for the electronics industry.

Our Vision

To be the leading environmental testing facility for electronic products in the industry and defense sector, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, while driving sustainable growth.

Our Mission

At AMETL, our mission is to provide comprehensive and reliable environmental
testing services for electronic products. We aim to support the customer by:

  • Delivering accurate and timely testing results
  • Fostering a culture of continuous improvement
  • Building strong partnerships with our clients
  • Upholding the highest standards of integrity and safety
  • Achieving sustainable growth

Why Choose Us ?

  • Expertise: Proficient in IS/ISO/IEC 17025:2017 auditing. Experts in electronics and electrical testing aligned with IS, IEC, JSS, and MIL standards.
  • State-of-the-Art Equipment: Equipped with cutting-edge chambers and vibration systems to simulate extreme environmental conditions with precision.
  • Comprehensive Services: Our capabilities include high-temperature testing, Low-temperature testing, high-altitude simulation, thermal cycling, thermal shock, damp heat testing, vibration testing and mechanical shock.
  • Customer Focused: We collaborate closely with clients to deliver tailored testing solutions that meet specific product and compliance goals.
  • Testing Rates: Competitive rates designed to offer value without compromising on quality or accuracy.
